## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, password, and account credentials
- **Profile Information**: Name, job title, company, career details, and professional background
- **Career Development Data**: Career goals, skills assessments, progress tracking, and personal reflections
- **Communication Data**: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our application, features used, and time spent
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
- **Performance Data**: Application performance, error logs, and system diagnostics
- **AI Interaction Data**: Coaching conversations, prompts, and AI-generated responses

### 2.3 Special Category Data

We process sensitive personal data that may include:
- Personal career aspirations and life goals
- Professional assessments and performance evaluations
- Intimate thoughts and reflections about career development
- Personal challenges and vulnerabilities shared during coaching
- Skill assessments and competency evaluations

We process this sensitive data only with your explicit consent and implement additional safeguards to protect it.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

- **Service Provision**: To provide and maintain our career development platform
- **AI Coaching**: To generate personalized coaching insights and career advice
- **Progress Tracking**: To help you monitor your career development journey
- **Account Management**: To manage your account, subscriptions, and billing

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes

- **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
- **Security**: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Support**: To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

### 3.3 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

| Purpose | Legal Basis | Data Types |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Account creation and management | Contract performance | Account information, profile data |
| AI coaching and insights | Explicit consent | Career data, reflections, AI interactions |

| Service improvement | Legitimate interest | Usage data, performance metrics |
| Security and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest | Technical data, usage patterns |

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with least privilege principles
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication and session management
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
- **Backup Security**: Encrypted backups with secure storage

### 5.2 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify relevant authorities within 72 hours of discovery
- Notify affected users without undue delay
- Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
- Conduct a thorough investigation and implement preventive measures

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

### 6.1 Access and Portability

- **Right of Access**: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- **Right to Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Data Export**: Export your data in JSON or Markdown formats anytime

### 6.2 Data Control

- **Right to Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Right to Restriction**: Request limitation of data processing
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain types of data processing

### 6.3 Consent and Withdrawal

- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of certain data processing activities
- **Marketing Preferences**: Control marketing communications

### 6.4 Automated Decision Making

- **Right to Human Review**: Request human review of AI-generated insights
- **Right to Explanation**: Understand how AI decisions are made
- **Right to Challenge**: Challenge automated decisions that affect you

### 6.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please [contact us](mailto:kasper.herloev@gmail.com). We will respond to your request within 30 days.
